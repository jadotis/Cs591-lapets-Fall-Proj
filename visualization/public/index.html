About This Visualization

Each red dot indicates the location of an individual who qualifies as "obese" due to the Massachusetts standards. This data is pulled daily from data.cityofboston.gov and is rendered on the map to the left. The button below when clicked runs the K-Means algorithm under constraints to place the optimal locations for healthy food stores (Represented by blue points). Feel free to drag the individuals around and run the simulation again! The data will be cleared and reset per each click of the button. You can also enter your address into the search field. If the address you entered is within the Boston, city limits, we will display some information about your area. Have fun!
